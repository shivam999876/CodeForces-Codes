The lengths of the strings corresponding to the T-shirt sizes do not exceed <span class="MathJax_Preview" style="color: inherit;"><span class="MJXp-math" id="MJXp-Span-23"><span class="MJXp-mn" id="MJXp-Span-24">50</span></span></span>$50$.
